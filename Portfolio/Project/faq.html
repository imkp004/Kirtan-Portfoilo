FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Time

  • If you are traveling then the first question will be what time of the year is best to travel?

  • The best time of the year is in Spring or fall season of the year because in summer it gets very hot so, it will take away your fun of traveling. It is warm and chilled aroung fall and spring and the temperature will be around 68°F to 77°F of that area. There are lot of festivals like Diwali, Christmas, and New Year to celebrate and watch the lighting and fireworks thus, it is the best time of the year to travel to India, Delhi.

Safety

  • When it comes to safety people often ask is it safe to travel as a family in the places around delhi?

  • Considering the popultation, and lot of sound you have to prepared accordingly because there will be all kinds of different people living there and there is sound which some people prefer a crowded place but others will not especially, people with their families.

  • How safe is Delhi?

  • It is very safe if you're precautious so, there are different animals you will find in the place such as cows, elephant, dogs, cat, camles, and sometimes snakes are very common. You will find those animals everywhere but no worries it dosen't harm people as far as you don't provoke it so, just enjoy the nature.

Money

  • How much it will cost to travel in Delhi?

  • Hotels are not much expensive compared to other places around the world so, you can stay in most expensive luxurious hotel with the cost of around $40 per night with breakfast, lunch and dinner included. The cost of flights will be around $400 - $600 per person and your average cost of the day at max will be $30. So, you can plan that accordingly to your budget

  • How to Money Exchange?

  • Delhi is popular for tourists so, they have banks and ATM's all over the place which accepts the debit cards, credit cards, or even the cash money to exchange for rupees but to do that you will need proper ID. Prefer using cards for the payment for just to be secure.
